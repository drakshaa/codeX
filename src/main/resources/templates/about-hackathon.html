Hackathon 2026: The National Coding Challenge

1. Introduction: About NIT Silchar

The National Institute of Technology, Silchar, established in 1967 as a Regional Engineering College, is one of India’s premier technical institutions. Upgraded to a National Institute of Technology in 2002 and declared an Institute of National Importance under the NIT Act of 2007, NIT Silchar stands out for its excellence in education, research, and innovation.

Recognitions & Highlights
  • Consistently ranked among the top engineering institutes in India: **NIRF Engineering Ranking of 40** and **Overall Category Ranking of 92 in 2024**.
  • Internationally recognized: **QS Asia University Ranking of 541 (2025)** and **205th in the Green Metric Global Ranking (2022)**.
  • Hub for advanced research in emerging technologies and active collaborations with national and international organizations.

2. Objective of the Event

This Hackathon aims to provide a national platform for students, researchers, and tech-enthusiasts to showcase their coding and problem-solving skills. Participants will tackle real-world problems using **Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and other emerging technologies.**

Key Goals:
  • Encourage innovation in AI/ML-driven solutions.
  • Foster collaborative problem-solving.
  • Identify top talent and creative thinkers in the tech community.
  • Provide exposure to real-life problem-solving environments.

3. Competition Details (3 Rounds)

Team Size: Up to Three individuals (Students from UG/PG/PhD backgrounds)

Round 1: Screening (Online)

Objective: MCQs, aptitude, and basic coding questions.

Mode: Online

Date: **January 2026 (Second Week)**

Qualification: Top performers securing **50% marks** or ranking in the **top 75%**.

Round 2: Coding Challenge (Online)

Objective: Problem statements based on coding and AI/ML application (Submission of PPT/Video).

Mode: Online

Date: **February 2026 (First Week)**

Top teams will be shortlisted for the final round.

Round 3: Final Hackathon (Offline)

Objective: Final hackathon challenge with real-time problem-solving.

Mode: Offline

Venue: **NIT Silchar, Assam**

Date: **February 2026 (Last Week)**
